Coinlytics Referral & Rewards Program Details

To help spread the word about Coinlytics, we’re offering a simple referral program – share your unique referral code with your friends. If they sign up for a Coinlytics subscription, we reward you both with some free Bitcoin delivered to you via the Lightning Network. No sign up required and we collect no personal information of any kind.Â 

1

Download and install Coinlytics. Coinlytics is free to use with subscription options for advanced features.

2

Navigate to More and tap on the banner at the top of the menu to see your unique referral link. Share your referral link with your friends using the Share button or copying and pasting your link and sending via email, iMessage, social networking, etc. You can refer as many friends as you like. When any of them subscribe, you'll get a referral reward.

3

Your friends need to download and install Coinlytics from the App Store, then tap on your referral link to populate your referral code into their app. They will see your referral code when they subscribe from the purchase screen.

4

As soon as they subscribe, you'll receive a notification that your referral code has been activated - open your app and navigate to More -> 'Rewards' and you'll see a new entry in the 'Active' section. Your friend will also see a similar entry in their app as well. Give our team 24 hours (usually less) for your reward to be activated.

5

Once activated, tap on the reward to see a QR Code that you can scan with your Lightning Wallet. You can also save the QR Code to your Photos to open up with your app, or you can copy/paste the payment link and then paste it into your Lightning Wallet. We've tested Wallet of Satoshi, Zebedee, Blue Wallet and Breez and these all work seamlessly. We cannot guarantee support on other lightning wallets but these should work.

6

If you have any problems, contact us. You can see a detailed tutorial on our Help portal.

For any questions about our rewards program or to report any issues, please contact us.

*Your Bitcoin reward is delivered via LN URL Withdrawals which are invoices that we prepay on the Lightning Network. A Lightning Wallet is required to receive the reward - once redeemed you can transfer those sats to any other lightning or Bitcoin wallet.
